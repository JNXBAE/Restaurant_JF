# GCAO Prompt – Jungle Foods Restaurant Website

## GOAL

Develop a **modern, responsive, and premium restaurant website** for **Jungle Foods** using only **HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript**. The website should provide an excellent customer experience while also including a secure **Admin Dashboard** where the restaurant owner can manage all restaurant content through complete CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations. The website should be designed as a real-world production-ready restaurant website with a unique jungle-inspired branding that reflects the restaurant's identity and can be upgraded to a backend in the future without changing the frontend architecture.
